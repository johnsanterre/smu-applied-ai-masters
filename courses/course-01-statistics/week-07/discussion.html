Week 7 Discussion Topics

Explore estimation concepts in the context of ML model evaluation.

Topic 1: Regularization as Bias-Variance Tradeoff

  • How does L2 regularization (ridge regression) introduce bias while reducing variance?
  • Why might a biased estimator sometimes be preferred in predictive modeling?
  • How does cross-validation help navigate the bias-variance tradeoff?

Topic 2: Confidence Intervals for Model Performance

  • How would you construct a confidence interval for a model's accuracy or F1-score?
  • Why are bootstrap confidence intervals often preferred in ML evaluation?
  • What are the challenges of reporting uncertainty in deep learning results?

Topic 3: Estimator Properties in Online Learning

  • How do online learning algorithms trade off bias and variance compared to batch methods?
  • What challenges arise when estimating streaming data statistics?
  • How do techniques like exponential moving averages balance recency with stability?
