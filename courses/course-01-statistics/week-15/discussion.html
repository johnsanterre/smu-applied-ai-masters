Week 15 Discussion Topics

Reflect on course themes and prepare for your capstone presentation.

Topic 1: Connecting Theory to Practice

  • Which course concept has been most valuable for your understanding of ML?
  • How has probabilistic thinking changed how you approach ML problems?
  • What concept remains most challenging and why?

Topic 2: The Future of Probabilistic ML

  • How are uncertainty quantification methods evolving in deep learning?
  • What role will Bayesian methods play as AI systems become more autonomous?
  • How might probabilistic reasoning improve AI safety and reliability?

Topic 3: Capstone Project Presentations

  • What modeling choices did you make and why?
  • How did you handle uncertainty in your analysis?
  • What would you do differently with more time or data?
