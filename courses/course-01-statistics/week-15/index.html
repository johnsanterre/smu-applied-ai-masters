Course Integration & Capstone

This final week synthesizes all course concepts through a capstone project applying statistical inference and probabilistic modeling to a real AI/ML problem.

Learning Objectives

  • Integrate probability, statistics, and inference concepts into a complete analysis
  • Apply appropriate probabilistic models to real-world data
  • Communicate statistical findings effectively
  • Critically evaluate model assumptions and limitations
  • Demonstrate proficiency in core course competencies

Capstone Project Requirements

  • Dataset: Choose or propose a dataset for analysis
  • Modeling: Apply at least two probabilistic modeling approaches
  • Inference: Include Bayesian and/or frequentist inference
  • Evaluation: Proper uncertainty quantification
  • Presentation: 10-minute presentation + written report
