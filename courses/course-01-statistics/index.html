Foundation Phase

Statistical Inference and Probabilistic Modeling

Master the mathematical foundations of AI including probability theory, statistical inference, Bayesian reasoning, and probabilistic graphical models essential for understanding machine learning.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply probability theory to model uncertainty in AI systems
  • Perform statistical inference and hypothesis testing on real-world datasets
  • Implement Bayesian reasoning for updating beliefs with new evidence
  • Construct and analyze probabilistic graphical models
  • Evaluate model performance using statistical metrics and validation techniques
  • Connect statistical foundations to machine learning algorithms
