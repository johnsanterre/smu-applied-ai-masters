Week 8 Discussion Topics

Examine the role and limitations of hypothesis testing in ML research.

Topic 1: The Replication Crisis and ML

  • Many fields are experiencing a "replication crisis." Is ML research at risk?
  • What practices (p-hacking, HARKing) undermine statistical validity in ML papers?
  • How should ML papers report results to improve reproducibility?

Topic 2: A/B Testing in Industry

  • How do companies like Google and Netflix use hypothesis testing in A/B experiments?
  • What are the challenges of running A/B tests at scale (peeking, interference)?
  • When might Bayesian A/B testing be preferred over frequentist methods?

Topic 3: Model Comparison and Statistical Testing

  • How should we statistically compare two ML models' performance?
  • What are the problems with using a single train/test split for comparison?
  • How does cross-validation affect the assumptions of standard t-tests?
