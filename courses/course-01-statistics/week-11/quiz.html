Week 11 Quiz: Markov Chains

Q1: The Markov property states that:

  • All states are independent
  • P(Xₙ₊₁|X₁,...,Xₙ) = P(Xₙ₊₁|Xₙ)
  • Future states are deterministic
  • Past and future are identically distributed

Q2: In a transition matrix P, each row must:

  • Sum to the number of states
  • Be orthogonal to other rows
  • Sum to 1
  • Contain only 0s and 1s

Q3: A stationary distribution π satisfies:

  • πP = π
  • Pπ = 0
  • All entries are zero
  • π = (1/n, 1/n, ..., 1/n) always

Q4: An ergodic Markov chain:

  • Has at least one absorbing state
  • Has periodic states
  • Cannot reach all states from any state
  • Has a unique stationary distribution that is the limiting distribution

Q5: A Hidden Markov Model differs from a Markov chain by:

  • Having no hidden states
  • Having hidden states that emit observations
  • Only having two states
  • Being continuous-time
