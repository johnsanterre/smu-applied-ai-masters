Week 6 Discussion Topics

Explore the implications of the CLT for modern machine learning and AI.

Topic 1: The CLT and Batch Size in Deep Learning

Batch size is a critical hyperparameter, and its effects relate directly to the CLT.

  • How does batch size affect gradient noise, and what are the tradeoffs between large and small batches?
  • Some research suggests small batches lead to better generalization. How might gradient noise contribute to this?
  • What is "gradient accumulation" and how does it interact with CLT principles?

Topic 2: When the CLT Fails

The CLT requires finite variance, which is not always satisfied.

  • What happens with heavy-tailed distributions (like Cauchy) that have infinite variance?
  • How might fat tails in financial returns affect ML models trained on such data?
  • What alternatives exist when CLT assumptions are violated?

Topic 3: Bootstrap Methods

Bootstrap resampling is a computational alternative to CLT-based inference.

  • When might bootstrap be preferred over CLT-based confidence intervals?
  • How does the bootstrap relate to the LLN and CLT conceptually?
  • What are the limitations of bootstrap in small samples?
