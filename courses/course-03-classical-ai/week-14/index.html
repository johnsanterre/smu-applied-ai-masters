From Classical to Modern AI

Bridge classical AI concepts to modern machine learning and neural approaches.

Learning Objectives

  • Compare symbolic vs subsymbolic AI
  • Understand the rise of machine learning
  • See how classical concepts appear in modern systems
  • Explore neuro-symbolic integration
  • Discuss the future of AI research

Key Concepts

  • Symbolic AI: Logic, rules, explicit knowledge
  • Subsymbolic: Neural networks, learned representations
  • Neuro-Symbolic: Combining both paradigms
  • Inductive Bias: Assumptions built into learning
