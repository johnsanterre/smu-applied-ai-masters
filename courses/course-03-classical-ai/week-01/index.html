Introduction to AI & Intelligent Agents

This week provides a historical perspective on AI and introduces the agent paradigm that organizes our study of intelligent systems.

Learning Objectives

  • Trace the history of AI from Turing to deep learning
  • Define intelligence and the goals of AI research
  • Describe the agent model: percepts, actions, goals, environment
  • Classify agent types by complexity
  • Identify PEAS descriptions for various agents

Key Concepts

  • Agent: Entity that perceives and acts
  • Rationality: Acting to maximize expected utility
  • PEAS: Performance, Environment, Actuators, Sensors
  • Task Environment: Properties like observability, determinism
