Local Search & Optimization

When finding the path doesn't matter, local search finds solutions by iteratively improving candidates.

Learning Objectives

  • Implement hill climbing and its variants
  • Apply simulated annealing
  • Understand genetic algorithms
  • Recognize local optima problems
  • Choose appropriate local search for problem type

Key Concepts

  • Hill Climbing: Move to best neighbor, greedy local
  • Local Optimum: No neighbor is better, but not global best
  • Simulated Annealing: Accept worse moves probabilistically
  • Genetic Algorithm: Population-based evolutionary search
