Planning Under Uncertainty

Make decisions when actions have probabilistic outcomes using MDPs and POMDPs.

Learning Objectives

  • Formulate Markov Decision Processes (MDPs)
  • Apply value iteration and policy iteration
  • Understand the Bellman equation
  • Introduce POMDPs for partial observability
  • Connect to reinforcement learning

Key Concepts

  • MDP: States, actions, transitions, rewards
  • Policy: Mapping from states to actions
  • Value Function: Expected cumulative reward
  • Bellman Equation: Recursive value definition
