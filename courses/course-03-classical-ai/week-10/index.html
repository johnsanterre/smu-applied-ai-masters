First-Order Logic

Extend propositional logic with objects, relations, and quantifiers for richer knowledge representation.

Learning Objectives

  • Write sentences with predicates and functions
  • Use universal (∀) and existential (∃) quantifiers
  • Apply unification for inference
  • Implement forward and backward chaining
  • Understand limitations of FOL

Key Concepts

  • Predicate: Property or relation, e.g., Loves(x, y)
  • Quantifiers: ∀x (for all x), ∃x (exists x)
  • Unification: Finding substitutions to make terms identical
  • Skolemization: Removing existential quantifiers
