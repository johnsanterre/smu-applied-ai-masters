Propositional Logic & Inference

Represent knowledge with logical sentences and derive new knowledge through inference.

Learning Objectives

  • Write knowledge in propositional logic
  • Build truth tables and determine validity
  • Apply inference rules (modus ponens, resolution)
  • Convert to CNF for resolution
  • Understand soundness and completeness

Key Concepts

  • Proposition: Statement that is true or false
  • Connectives: ∧ (and), ∨ (or), ¬ (not), → (implies)
  • Entailment: KB ⊨ α means α is true in all models of KB
  • Resolution: Complete inference for propositional logic
