Decision Trees & Ensemble Methods

Build interpretable tree models and understand how ensembles improve performance.

Learning Objectives

  • Train decision trees for classification and regression
  • Understand splitting criteria (Gini, entropy, MSE)
  • Control overfitting with pruning
  • Visualize and interpret tree decisions
  • Introduce bagging and ensemble concepts

Key Concepts

  • Splitting: Choose feature/threshold maximizing information gain
  • Gini Impurity: Measures class distribution at node
  • Pruning: max_depth, min_samples_leaf to prevent overfitting
  • Bagging: Train on bootstrap samples, average predictions
