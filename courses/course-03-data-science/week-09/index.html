Clustering & K-Means

Discover natural groupings in data without labeled examples.

Learning Objectives

  • Implement K-Means clustering
  • Choose optimal K using elbow and silhouette
  • Apply hierarchical clustering
  • Use DBSCAN for density-based clustering
  • Evaluate clustering quality

Key Concepts

  • K-Means: Minimize within-cluster variance
  • Elbow Method: Plot inertia vs K
  • Silhouette: Measure cluster cohesion and separation
  • DBSCAN: Density-based, handles outliers
