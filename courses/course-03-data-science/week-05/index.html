Model Evaluation & Cross-Validation

Properly evaluate models and avoid common pitfalls in performance estimation.

Learning Objectives

  • Implement k-fold cross-validation
  • Choose appropriate metrics for your problem
  • Build and interpret confusion matrices
  • Understand precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC
  • Avoid data leakage in evaluation

Key Concepts

  • K-Fold CV: Split data into K folds, train/test K times
  • Confusion Matrix: TP, FP, TN, FN counts
  • Precision: TP / (TP + FP) - accuracy of positives
  • Recall: TP / (TP + FN) - coverage of positives
