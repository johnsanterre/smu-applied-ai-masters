Dimensionality Reduction & PCA

Reduce feature dimensions while preserving important patterns.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply PCA for dimensionality reduction
  • Interpret principal components and explained variance
  • Use t-SNE and UMAP for visualization
  • Understand when dimensionality reduction helps
  • Apply to image compression and noise reduction

Key Concepts

  • PCA: Project onto directions of maximum variance
  • Explained Variance: How much info each component captures
  • t-SNE: Non-linear, preserves local structure
  • UMAP: Faster alternative to t-SNE
