Frequently Asked Questions

Program Structure

How long does it take to complete the program?

The program consists of 10 courses. Full-time students typically complete the program in 2 years, while part-time students may take 3-4 years.

Are there prerequisite requirements?

Students should have a bachelor's degree and basic familiarity with mathematics. The Foundation Phase courses (1-3) are designed to establish the necessary technical background.

Can I take courses out of order?

Courses are designed to build upon each other. We recommend following the sequence, especially within each phase. Some flexibility exists with advisor approval.

Technical Requirements

What software/hardware do I need?

A laptop capable of running Python, Jupyter notebooks, and web browsers. Cloud computing resources are provided for intensive computational work.

What programming languages are used?

Python is the primary language. You'll also gain exposure to SQL, JavaScript, and infrastructure tools like Docker.

Assessments & Projects

How are courses graded?

Each course includes quizzes, programming assignments, projects, and participation. The final capstone involves building an AI product.
