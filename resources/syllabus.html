Masters in Applied AI — Full Program Syllabus

This comprehensive program spans 10 courses, each with a 15-week curriculum designed to build progressive expertise in applied artificial intelligence.

Program Phases

Foundation Phase (Courses 1-3)

Establish the mathematical, computational, and classical AI fundamentals required for advanced study.

  • Course 1: Statistical Inference and Probabilistic Modeling
  • Course 2: Introduction to Programming and Computational Thinking
  • Course 3: Classical Artificial Intelligence: Search and Logic

Core AI Phase (Courses 4-6)

Dive deep into modern AI technologies including LLMs, prompt engineering, and scalable systems.

  • Course 4: Intent-Driven Software Engineering and Prompt Design
  • Course 5: Large Language Model Architectures and Deep Learning
  • Course 6: Distributed Systems and Cloud Infrastructure for AI

Advanced Phase (Courses 7-8)

Apply AI to business transformation and autonomous systems development.

  • Course 7: Business Cases for Transformation through AI
  • Course 8: Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Orchestration

Capstone Phase (Courses 9-10)

Integrate human-centered design with product development to create real-world AI solutions.

  • Course 9: Human-AI Interaction and Design Storytelling
  • Course 10: AI-Native Product Incubation

Academic Calendar

Each course runs for 15 weeks, including instruction, projects, and assessments. Students complete the program in approximately 2-3 years depending on enrollment pace.
