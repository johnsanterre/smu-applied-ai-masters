A comprehensive 10-course graduate program preparing leaders for the AI-driven future

Program Overview

The Masters in Applied Artificial Intelligence program at SMU combines rigorous technical foundations with practical, hands-on experience. Students progress through four learning phases, building from foundational concepts to advanced specialization and culminating in real-world product development.

Program Structure

  • Foundation Phase: Courses 1-3 establish core mathematical, programming, and AI fundamentals
  • Core AI Phase: Courses 4-6 dive deep into LLMs, distributed systems, and AI engineering
  • Advanced Phase: Courses 7-8 explore business transformation and autonomous systems
  • Capstone Phase: Courses 9-10 focus on human-AI design and product incubation
